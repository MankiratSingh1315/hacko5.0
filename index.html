About HackOWASP

Catchy Tagline

HackOwasp is an annual national hackathon that is organized by the members of the esteemed OWASP Student Chapter, Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology, Patiala. The fifth edition of HackOwasp promises to be bigger and better than ever before. It is an excellent opportunity for participants to subsume their creativity with ambition and give rise to innovation in the burgeoning world of technology. The event has gained national recognition and stands tall on the global scale. The primary aim of HackOwasp is to motivate students to push the boundaries of technology and go beyond the possible.We encourage all participants to take advantage of this exciting opportunity and be a part of this incredible journey of innovation and creativity. .

About OWASP

The Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology (TIET) has consistently produced highly skilled talent for the nation and abroad, pioneering engineering education, research, and innovation. The OWASP Student Chapter, a prestigious coding society, comprises ingenious problem-solvers who are eager to enhance the world through innovative techniques and discoveries. Through successfully organizing numerous hackathons, tech-talks, workshops, and coding nights, we have always endeavored to maintain a thriving coding culture throughout the campus.
